Derek Fitzpatrick Sneed is an artist and designer based in Oakland.

As a child he would dissect the small lizards that entered into his dreams. They would sing to him as he disassembled their underlying substruct. It was through their songs that he came to know The Absolute.

Bordering on the multidisciplinary, Derek's work is derived from the intersections of:

Art History / Brand / Urban Design / Typography / Nondualism / 90s Television / Information Security / Robotics / Data Visualization / Vampires / Demoscene / Anime / Classic Country / “Memes” / Intersectionality / Comics / User Xperience / Post-Facebook / Analytic Philosophy / Digital Archiving / Virtual Reality / Sociology / Crypts / Cryptocurrency / Video Games / Post-Humanism / Richard Stallman / Artificial Intelligence / Neo-Marxism
